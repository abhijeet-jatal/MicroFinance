Our Services

These are the type of loans we provide

Personal Loan

Personal Loan is an unsecured credit provided by financial institutions based on criteria like employment history, repayment capacity, income level, profession and credit history.

Business Loan

A business loan loan is borrowed money that businesses use to cover costs they can't afford on their own in the short term.

Home Loan

A home loan is a secured loan that is obtained to purchase a property by offering it as collateral.

Vehicle Loan

Vehicle Loans are secured loans, i.e., the purchased vehicle serves as collateral with the lender until you repay the loan in full.

Eligibility

Personal Loan:

Turn your dreams into reality with a Personal Loan from Bank. Take that vacation you have been delaying or complete the home renovation that has been pending. With an Bank Personal Loan , you can do it all.


Individuals who can take a Personal Loan:

  • Salaried Employees

    • Employees of public and private limited companies

    • Government sector employees including Public Sector Undertakings, Central and Local bodies

  • Minimum age of 21 years

  • Maximum age of 60 years at the time of maturity of the Personal Loan

  • Minimum net monthly income – Rs. 15,000

Documents required for Personal Loan

  • Duly filled application form

  • KYC documents

  • Loan agreement duly signed and Standing Instruction (SI) Request / ECS Forms
